Drain & Sewer Line Repair and Replacement

We provide complete drain and sewer line services, from accurate diagnostics to targeted repairs and full system replacement when needed. Whether you're dealing with slow drains, recurring backups, or a main sewer line issue, we identify the root cause and recommend clear, practical solutions—not temporary fixes.

Your drain and sewer system plays a critical role in keeping your home clean, safe, and functioning properly. Over time, pipes can become clogged, damaged, or deteriorated due to grease buildup, tree root intrusion, shifting soil, or aging materials.

Our services include

  • Drain & sewer line repair and replacement
  • Drain cleaning and clog removal
  • Sewer camera inspection and precise diagnostics
  • Spot repairs for damaged pipe sections
  • Trenchless repair and replacement options (when applicable)
  • Dependable Sewer Ejector Pump Services
  • EBMUD PSL Compliance

Precision Work, Built to Last

We use modern inspection tools and proven methods to ensure every job is handled with precision and care.
