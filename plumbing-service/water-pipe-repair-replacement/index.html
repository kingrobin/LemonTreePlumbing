Water Pipe Repair & Replacement

Your home's water piping system is essential for delivering clean, reliable water to every fixture. Over time, pipes can wear out, corrode, leak, or lose pressure — leading to water damage, reduced performance, and higher utility costs if not addressed properly.

We provide professional water pipe repair and replacement services, with a focus on accurate diagnosis and long-term solutions. Whether you're dealing with a visible leak, low water pressure, or aging piping, we identify the root cause and recommend the most effective approach.

Our services include

  • Water leak detection and pipe repair
  • Replacement of damaged or corroded pipes
  • Partial repiping for problem areas
  • Full home repiping (when necessary)
  • Pipe rerouting and system upgrades (PRV, ball valves, loops)
  • Smart Water Shut-Off Valve installation (Moen Flo)
  • Main shut-off replacement
  • Main water line repair & replacement
  • Water line repairs
  • Slab leak repair

Precision Work & Reliable Performance

Proper pipe installation requires careful planning, correct sizing, and attention to detail. We use quality materials and proven installation methods to ensure your plumbing system operates efficiently and meets current code standards. When repairs are needed, we focus on durable solutions — not temporary fixes — so you can avoid recurring leaks and unexpected issues. Whether upgrading older galvanized piping or repairing a localized issue, our goal is a clean, dependable system that supports your home for years to come.
