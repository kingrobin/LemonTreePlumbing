Gas Line Installation, Repair & Replacement

Gas lines are a critical part of your home's safety and functionality. Whether supplying water heaters, furnaces, stoves, or outdoor appliances, properly installed and maintained gas lines are essential for safe and reliable operation.

We provide professional gas line installation, repair, and replacement services, using proven methods and quality materials to ensure long-term performance. Whether you're adding a new appliance, upgrading an existing system, or addressing a leak or pressure issue, we diagnose the situation accurately and deliver the right solution.

Our services include

  • New gas line installation for appliances and home additions
  • Gas leak detection and repair
  • Replacement of aging or damaged gas piping
  • Gas pressure testing and system evaluation
  • Code-compliant upgrades and corrections

Earthquake Gas Shut-Off Valve Installation

In the East Bay, seismic safety isn't optional — it's essential. We install automatic earthquake gas shut-off valves designed to immediately stop gas flow in the event of significant seismic activity, helping protect your home from fire and structural risk. Each installation is completed with proper placement, calibration, and system testing to ensure reliable performance when it matters most.
