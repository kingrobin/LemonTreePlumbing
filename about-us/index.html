Meet the Team Behind LemonTree Plumbing

LemonTree Plumbing is a locally owned, service-focused plumbing company serving the East Bay. We specialize in helping homeowners and small businesses keep their plumbing systems safe, efficient, and worry-free—without surprise charges or high-pressure sales.

When you call us, you get a real person, clear communication, and a licensed plumber who takes the time to understand your problem and explain the best solution.

Our Story

LemonTree Plumbing was built on a simple idea: treat every home like it's your own. After a decade in the plumbing industry, Ramon Lin founded LemonTree to do things the way he believes they should be done — show up on time, explain the problem in plain language, and only recommend work that's truly needed. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted East Bay plumbing team serving 9 cities. But the approach hasn't changed: honest assessments, upfront pricing, and the kind of care that keeps customers coming back.
